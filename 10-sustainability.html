Source Reduction, the most
impactful approach to recycling.

The unfortunate reality is that while humans create 300 million tons of plastic waste each year, only 15% is actually recycled. That means approximately 255 million tons of recyclable plastic is entering our landfills and oceans every year. That’s why AeroFlexx is tackling this issue head-on by reducing our use of plastic at the source.

  • Using 50% less plastic up front reduces our carbon footprint in the end – a lighter package lowers energy consumption and supply chain requirements!
  • Less weight to ship, less transportation required, less emissions.
  • Unbreakable design eliminates triple packaging requirements
    producing less E-commerce waste.
  • Reducing the amount of plastic we use at the source leads to over a 40% reduction in plastic being introduced in the environment.

Don’t just recycle, TerraCycle.

AeroFlexx is created with a commitment to sustainability and our packaging is 100% recycle-ready. Thanks to our partnership with TerraCycle, consumers can recycle AeroFlexx packages via a free and easy mail-in method.

Request

Consumers access the recycling envelope program page on TerraCycle’s website to request a recycling envelope.

Distribute

TerraCycle distributes a recycling envelope to the consumer. A pre-paid USPS shipping label is printed directly on the envelope.

Collect/Send

Consumers fill the recycling envelope with your waste. When the envelope is full, the consumer drops it into any USPS mailbox to be sent to TerraCycle.

Recycle

TerraCycle receives the envelopes and recycles the waste inside into new plastic products.

2025 Vision

Moving forward, AeroFlexx is partnering with industry leaders and suppliers to provide a breakthrough in recycle-ready and curbside recyclable plastic films that are compatible with our technology and manufacturing process.
