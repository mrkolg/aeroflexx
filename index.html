MANUFACTURERS

Traditional Bottles

Consumers

  • Cap - consumer has to open/close
  • Potentially could have Non-controlled/precise dispensing (depending on cap/lack of cap)
  • 2 handed operation (not as convenient to use)
  • More mess - gunky cap
  • Could leak or spill if cap is not secure

AFX Packaging

Consumers

  • No caps - one step easy tear
  • Precision/Controlled dispensing
  • One handed operation
  • No gunky residue
  • Self sealing valve/won't spill
